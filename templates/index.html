    Cow Cure

    Welcome to Cow Cure! Cow Cure is an initiative taken up by Poojya Gupta, Dikshita Bargoda and Bhavya Parmar. We at Cow Cure have a vision to make farmers self-dependent by providing a disease guide which helps the farmer to identify potential health issues in their cattle and suggests the suitable treatments and necessary precautions. We hope to make the world a better place for cows by our small efforts.
